About

I am a College of Engineering Fellow and Ron and Wanda Crockett Endowed Fellow at the University of Washington, where my PhD research focuses on building 3D stem cell systems for studying tissue regeneration microenvironments.

My background spans the full arc from wet lab science and hardware fabrication to business strategy and consulting. I believe the most impactful innovations emerge at the intersection of rigorous science and clear-eyed market thinking.

As Entrepreneurship Lead for iGEM Rochester, I moved a Gold Medal-winning dual-channel 3D bioprinting system from the bench to a complete business plan — conducting customer discovery, mapping biomanufacturing bottlenecks, and presenting at the Grand Jamboree in Paris, outcompeting 400+ global teams.

Research & Projects

My work spans biomedical engineering, synthetic biology, finite element analysis, and entrepreneurship. Selected projects across my research career.

PhD Research
3D Stem Cell Systems for Tissue Regeneration
Building 3D stem cell systems to study tissue regeneration microenvironments at the University of Washington. 2023
iGEM · Gold Medal
Dual-Channel 3D Bioprinting System for Biomanufacturing
Developed a parallel culture dual-channel 3D bioprinter using synthetically engineered E. coli and S. cerevisiae. Designed hardware extrusion components in Onshape, conducted tensile testing on alginate structures, and analyzed mechanical properties using MATLAB. Led entrepreneurship track: conducted customer discovery across the biomanufacturing supply chain and developed a complete business plan.

2022 – 2025
Biomechanics
Macromolecular Infiltration & Synthetic Cartilage Mechanics
Investigated how infiltration of albumin-sized macromolecules and microspheres affects the permeability of agarose hydrogels. Developed custom 3D-printed indentation hardware and linear regression algorithms with iterative optimization. Modeled material behavior using MATLAB and FEBio.
Published · BMES Annual Meeting, Seattle 2023
2024 – 2025
Medical Devices
Ergonomic Surgical Pedal System
Led SOLIDWORKS design of a foot-controlled surgical pedal system to enhance surgeon comfort and reduce operative fatigue. Integrated machine learning and Arduino for real-time wireless operation. Coordinated usability testing with surgeons and aligned design documentation with FDA Class II medical device standards.
2024
Finite Element Analysis
Osteoporosis & Fracture Risk in the Proximal Femur
Developed four finite element models of the proximal femur in Abaqus CAE to investigate how osteoporosis alters strain distribution during walking. Systematically varied cortical thickness and material properties from healthy to increasingly osteoporotic conditions.
2023
Mechanical Design
Baja SAE Suspension Design & Validation
Redesigned Baja car suspension geometry for the 2023 competition season using SIEMENS NX. Performed load transfer and finite element analysis to ensure stress limits. New front upper A-arm design achieved 40% weight reduction at equivalent stress tolerance.
40% weight reduction
